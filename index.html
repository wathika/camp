What is Comp Camp!?

Comp Camp(Computer Camp) Is an ICT-Driven platform that seeks to front the spread of digital literacy in kids in rural and marginalized communities. This Event is organized by AFRICAN CENTRUNION a social intervention initiative that aims at using knowledge transfer in ICT and Entreprenuership to fight Africa's big3: Ignorance, Poverty & Sickness!
